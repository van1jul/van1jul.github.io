About Me
My name is Vanessa Wan. I graduated from UBC with a major in
Computer Science. I was born and raised in a little paradise island
called Mauritius and left to study abroad in another surreal
wonderland also known as Vancouver, BC.
Depending on what I want to achieve, I love working with Adobe
Illustrator/Photoshop/XD/Premiere Pro, Sketch, InVision, Axure,
HTML5, CSS3 and Javascript… And a hand mixer for when I am baking.
I love the thinking process it takes to actualize what I envision—
whether that be recreating a pumpkin pie from a recipe or building and
refining an interface from user requirements.
Revealing my work to an audience is the most exhilarating part of this
job as I thrive off of critique. I see it as an opportunity for
reflection and growth, and of course when the Gordon Ramsay of users
likes my work, it is only the cherry on top.
Designing isn't a piece of cake but it is most certainly a piece of cake
that I appreciate every bite of.

Feel free to follow or take a peek at my social media :)

Portfolio

Improved UBC Course Registration

A group project for our Human Computer Interaction class inspired by our collective frustration with the current UBC Course Registration website. The system required students to open multiple windows to figure out which course to take. We wanted to create an improved registration process that would allow students to plan their degree in an organized manner. As the lead designer, I decided to use Axure because it excels at prototyping complex interactions.
