About Me
    My name is Vanessa Wan. I graduated from UBC with a major in Computer Science. I was born and raised in a little paradise island called Mauritius and left to study abroad in another surreal wonderland also known as Vancouver, BC.
    Depending on what I want to achieve, I love working with Adobe Illustrator/Photoshop/XD/Premiere Pro, Sketch, InVision, Axure, HTML5, CSS3 and Javascript… And a hand mixer for when I am baking.
    I love the thinking process it takes to actualize what I envision—whether that be recreating a pumpkin pie from a recipe or building and refining an interface from user requirements.
    Revealing my work to an audience is the most exhilarating part of this job as I thrive off of critique. I see it as an opportunity for reflection and growth, and of course when the Gordon Ramsay of users likes my work, it is only the cherry on top.
    Designing isn't a piece of cake but it is most certainly a piece of cake that I appreciate every bite of.
