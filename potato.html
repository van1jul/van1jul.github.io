OVERVIEW
Get through it together
Exercising by yourself can be demotivating at times as you are not held accountable. However, knowing that you are not alone can motivate you to work out more and you don't want to let the others down.
Support each other
Through the activity feed, users can send short messages of encouragement to their friends who are having a hard time maintaining their average level of steps per day.
Show others you are the best team
The Rankings will motivate you and your buddies to keep up with the other teams or even be the best team of your area.
Challenge others as a team or solo
Challenge others to win points/awards and climb up the ladder. Be the best team!
Keep track of your progress
You will also be able to keep track of yourself with steps taken during the day, calories burned, calories eaten, distance walked and so on. You will also have a graph to see your progress during the day.
We won't let you down! (and neither should you)
I'm a Potato will remind you to get off your buttocks if you haven't been active all day. Reaching your goal each day will allow you to level up your potato. Show off to your friends that you are not a couch potato anymore!
